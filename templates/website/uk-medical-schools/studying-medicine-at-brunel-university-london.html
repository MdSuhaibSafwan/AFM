Study Medicine at Brunel University in London

This is one of the newest medical schools in the UK with their first intake in Sept 2022. Itis an excellent choice for international students that want to study medicine in the London- one of the world's most vibrant cities. It also offers one of the most generous international medical student scholarships totalling up to £300,000 GBP in it's first intake.

Get mentored by medical students at Brunel University London

Brunel’s new five-year MBBS degree offers clinical exposure from the early on in the course through placements across South East England and Greater London. The course establishes a team-based learning approach through small-group work and communication skills are developed from the very beginning due to the importance of this skill in becoming a compassionate doctor. The course is divided into three phases.

Phase 1 (first and second year) involves learning the fundamental biomedical science underlying medical practice through a systems-based approach. Basic clinical skills are also developed and students explore ethics, professionalism and law early on.Students can also undertake special study topics which can include an opportunity to take part in a research project or explore a field of interest within medicine more.

In Phase 2 (third and fourth year), clinical education expands through numerous clinical placements across different primary and secondary care settings. In the third year in particular, students explore different medical and surgical specialties. In the fourth year, students are taught holistic patient management and enhance their clinical experience and understanding.

Phase 3 (final year) focuses on preparing for the foundation programme and becoming a junior doctor through apprenticeships as well as elective period to explore medicine in different settings. Students also further develop and consolidate their clinical skills and their interpersonal and multidisciplinary teamwork skills.
