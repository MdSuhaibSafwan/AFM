FAQs

Is the TAG platform 100% free to start using?

Yes, registration and being a member of TAG is free. You will also have a free discovery call with a member of the TAG Team who will guide you through the options you have as a Pakistani student wanting to study university and work overseas.

How will I get university admission abroad?

Our university TAG mentors will pass their experience, special tips and advice (including their mistakes so you avoid them!) making your university application journey as simple as possible. Since many of them are also from Pakistan they will understand your needs and suggest best ways to complete your university application form.

After submitting your completed university application form, a TAG mentor will optimise and submit it to suitable universities from around the world allowing them to make contact with you and consider making you an university admission offer (with or without Scholarship).

How do I secure employment abroad?

Once you are enrolled into a university abroad via TAG, there could also be student employment opportunities available to you.

Before reaching the end of your university course, you will have your CV/resume checked and updated regularly as part of being a TAG member. This CV is then bought to the attention of employers from around the world who can make contact with you directly once you are a TAG Mentor.

This way, your chances of securing employment abroad are maximised even before completing the university course.

Do you help with Visas and IELTS requirements?

Yes, IELTS scores are usually higher since you will be mentored and tutored by other Pakistani university students who are currently living abroad. So they can guide and coach you in a way that helps you achieve the best IELTS scores possible. Once you accept an offer from a university abroad, the visa process for Pakistani students can start and we can provide access to guidance on all the steps required.

Will I also be eligible for scholarships to study abroad?

Yes, depending on your financial status, when universities from around the world see your application, they can also consider you for a scholarship too. This will save you a huge amount of time and effort searching and applying for each university individually.
