Medical school admission blogs

Medical students are some of the most resourceful students at university. Here are some resources some of our medical admission Alumni have put together. They are based on view and opinions before applying to medical school and also how these may have changed while studying.

You can reach out to them for personal advice and as so many of them are experienced tutors, they can help you get the grades you need too!

Please note that these blogs are from small number of our medical school application Alumni. Once you have joined the TAG community, you will have access to Alumni from medical schools around the world. They will be able to Alumni and guide you and share their personal experiences (that they might not have added onto a blog) to help you optimise your application to medical school.

You may have already applied and need assistance with interviews or MMI's and even choose which medical school to accept once you have received multiple offers!
